🛡️ Immediate Safety Steps

  • 1
    Remove any means of self-harm from your immediate environment
  • 2
    Call a trusted friend, family member, or mental health professional
  • 3
    Go to a safe place with other people around
  • 4
    Use grounding techniques: 5 things you see, 4 you hear, 3 you touch
  • 5
    If safe to do so, contact emergency services or go to nearest hospital

⚠️ Warning Signs to Watch For

Talking about death or suicide
Feeling hopeless or trapped
Severe mood swings
Withdrawing from activities
Giving away possessions
Increased substance use
Sleeping too much/little
Feeling like a burden
