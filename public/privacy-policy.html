🔒 Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 10, 2025

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At Mindful AI Ecosystem, your mental health data is sacred. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your personal information remains secure. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

🛡️ Data Minimization Principle: We only collect information necessary to provide you with personalized mental health support.
  • Account Information: Name, email, basic demographic information
  • Wellness Data: Assessment responses, practice sessions, mood check-ins
  • Interaction Data: AI conversation logs (anonymized), community posts (if you choose to share)
  • Usage Analytics: Feature usage patterns to improve our services

How We Use Your Information

  • Personalization: Customize your wellness journey and AI responses
  • Progress Tracking: Monitor your mental health journey over time
  • Safety: Identify crisis situations and provide appropriate support
  • Improvement: Enhance our AI models and platform features
  • Communication: Send important updates and wellness reminders

Data Protection Measures

🔐 Enterprise-Grade Security: Your data is protected with industry-leading security measures.
  • Encryption: All data encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access Controls: Strict role-based access to your information
  • Anonymization: Personal identifiers removed from analytics data
  • Local Storage: Sensitive data stored locally on your device when possible
  • Regular Audits: Continuous security assessments and improvements

Your Rights and Control

  • Data Access: Request a copy of all your personal data
  • Data Correction: Update or correct any inaccurate information
  • Data Deletion: Request complete removal of your account and data
  • Data Portability: Export your data in a readable format
  • Opt-Out: Withdraw consent for data processing at any time

AI and Machine Learning

Our AI systems process your data to provide personalized support. We use multiple safeguards:

  • AI models trained on anonymized, aggregated data
  • No personal conversations shared with third-party AI providers
  • Local processing prioritized over cloud processing
  • Human oversight for all AI-generated crisis interventions

Community Features

When participating in community features:

  • All posts are anonymous by default
  • Content moderation protects community safety
  • You control what information you share
  • Community data is separate from your personal wellness data

Third-Party Services

We partner with trusted services while maintaining your privacy:

  • AI Providers: OpenAI, Google Gemini (anonymized requests only)
  • Analytics: Aggregated usage data for platform improvement
  • Crisis Support: Integration with professional mental health services

Legal Compliance

We comply with all applicable privacy laws including GDPR, HIPAA guidelines, and regional mental health regulations. We may disclose information only when legally required or to prevent imminent harm.
